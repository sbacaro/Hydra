Hydra is installed 🎛️

The Hydra Virtual Soundcard is now available in your audio settings.

  1. Open Hydra from Applications.
  2. Allow the background helper in Login Items if prompted.
  3. To capture app audio, allow System Audio Recording when asked (System Settings → Privacy & Security).

Open Audio MIDI Setup to confirm the “Hydra Virtual Soundcard” device (256 in / 256 out).
