Hydra — Virtual Audio Patch Bay

This installer will set up Hydra on your Mac:

  • Hydra.app in your Applications folder.
  • The Hydra Virtual Soundcard driver, so the device appears in audio settings.

After installation, open Hydra to route audio between apps, devices, plugins and the network (AES67/NDI) in a single matrix.

Administrator privileges are required to install the audio driver. macOS will reload its audio engine during installation.
